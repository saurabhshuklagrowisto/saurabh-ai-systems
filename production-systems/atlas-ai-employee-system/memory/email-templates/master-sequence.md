# Master Cold Email Sequence — v1 (draft, not yet tested)

Updated: 2026-07-10 · Owner: Scribe (personalizes per lead), humans approve first 200 sends.
Personalization inputs: `{job_title}` from the actual posting, `{company}`, `{first_name}`, `{pain_point}` inferred from the posting (e.g. "high call volume"), `{ehr}` if named.

Rules: under 120 words per email · one idea per email · no images/links in touch 1–2 (deliverability) · plain conversational tone, no hype words ("revolutionary", "game-changer") · always reference the SPECIFIC job posting · CAN-SPAM footer + physical address + working unsubscribe on every send.

## Touch 1 — Day 0 (the posting)
Subject: `your {job_title} opening`

> Hi {first_name} — saw {company} is hiring a {job_title}.
>
> Quick thought before you fill it: practices your size are increasingly staffing that exact seat with a trained, HIPAA-trained virtual assistant in the Philippines — same duties ({duty_1}, {duty_2}), fully remote, at roughly 40–60% less than a local hire. {ehr_line}
>
> Worth a 15-minute look at how it works? Happy to share what a {job_title} placement looks like for a practice like yours.

`{ehr_line}` = "Since you're on {ehr}, everything they'd touch is already cloud-based." (omit if no EHR named)

## Touch 2 — Day 3 (proof)
Subject: `re: your {job_title} opening`
Short: one anonymized case study with numbers (hours saved, $ saved, ramp time). Question close: "Would numbers like that move the needle for {company}?"

## Touch 3 — Day 7 (objection preempt)
Subject: `the "but patients want to talk to someone local" thing`
Address the #1 objection head-on: US-hours coverage, neutral accent, dedicated (not shared) staff, HIPAA training + BAA. One line each.

## Touch 4 — Day 12 (cost math)
Subject: `quick math on that {job_title} seat`
Local fully-loaded cost vs. our monthly rate for the same duty list. One table or three lines. CTA: 15-min call.

## Touch 5 — Day 18 (breakup)
Subject: `closing the loop`
"Sounds like the timing isn't right — I'll leave you with this one-pager. If the {job_title} search drags or the backlog grows, we can usually have someone trained and live in 2 weeks." Stop sequence.
