# Generative AI Video Sample

A sample video produced with a generative AI video tool, shared here as a demo of generative AI video output.

- **Stream on Google Drive (anyone with the link can view):** https://drive.google.com/file/d/1VjmCcEbmgoFaIVpSrhFtzmsAb5mtMnu_/view?usp=sharing

## Built with

- **Kling AI** — video generation
- **Google Veo 3** (via Gemini) — video generation
- **ElevenLabs** — AI voice / audio
- **Clipchamp** — editing and assembly

## Notes

- The clip was generated on a **paid tier** of a generative AI video platform, so the tool's logo/watermark appears in the output as that plan's default branding.
- The inputs behind it (prompts, reference data, assets) can be recreated to regenerate similar output and adjusted to work accordingly — this is a demonstration of the workflow, not a final branded deliverable.
